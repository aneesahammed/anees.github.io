I was not a regular coffee☕️ person before coming here in Ireland. The plausible cause could be because I come from Dubai 🏙. Yes, I’ve spent a good 8 years of my life there. Gradually I started blending into the coffee☕️ culture of Ireland. Morning coffee from a nearby coffee-shop, another one at the office before getting into the routine agile sprint, it was much needed to keep me awake👀 ;) and last but not the least one at seven-wonders while walking back to the apartment. All of them handed their Loyalty paper punch cards to me. The number of loyalty cards I carry kept on growing. I still do carry my bulky wallet with all these cards.

In the very first week here in Ireland this idea💡 of an app to ease this pain point just popped into my head. As typical of me, I noted the workflow on my iPhone Note-app 📝 and started wondering why nobody addressed this yet.

As time has gone by I have become more focussed on my daily work, since I was quite new to this part of the world and as a first member of EMEA DevOps team I followed the strategy of “keep your head down and work hard!!”.

Finally, I started realising the natural itch of working on something my own and thought of keeping some time to work on this which was hanging on the list of side-project ideas for quite a long time.

So here it is, my humble effort to materialize the very idea of upgrading your good old paper loyalty cards. It is still work in progress, hoping to release it soon on apple app-store and android play store.

🌐Website: https://snapcircle.io/
