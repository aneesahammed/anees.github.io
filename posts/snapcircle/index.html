I was not a regular coffee☕️ person before coming here in Ireland🇮🇪. The plausible cause could be that I have spent a good 8 years of my life in Dubai🏙. Gradually I started blending into the coffee☕️ culture of Ireland - a morning coffee from nearby coffee-shop, another one at the office before getting into the routine agile sprint, it was much needed to keep me awake👀 ;). Last but not least a coffee from seven-wonders while walking back to my apartment.

With too many coffees came numerous Loyalty paper punch cards, that gave way to a bulky wallet!. I started wondering why nobody has addressed this issue yet.

So, my very first week in Ireland🇮🇪 prompted the idea💡 of an app to ease this pain point. As is very typical of me, I started noting the workflow on my iPhone Note-app 📝 and started wondering why nobody has addressed this yet.

As time went by I had to focus on my daily work, Being quite new to this part of the world and the first member of EMEA DevOps team, I followed the strategy of “keep your head down and work hard”.

Finally, I started realising the natural itch of working on something of my own and found some time to work on this app which was hanging on the list of side-project ideas for quite a long time.

So here it is, my humble effort to materialize the very idea of upgrading your good old paper loyalty cards. It is still work in progress, hoping to release it soon on apple app-store and android play store.

🌐Website: https://snapcircle.io/
