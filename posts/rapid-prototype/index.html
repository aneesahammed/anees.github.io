Rapid Prototyping

Aug 20, 2017 11:36 · 254 words · 2 minute read

This is to document my last remote consulting experience for a Mobile Application.

In order to get clarity in design, we often go through a prototype phase. This is an important phase as this allows you to refine the user experience.

Although there are a huge number of tools out there that are dedicated solely to prototyping. I’ve seen effective prototypes made with PowerPoint, Keynote, Acrobat, and Word. If you’re a designer and have skills in programs like high-end graphic software like Sketch. Or if you know HTML and CSS, you’re ahead of the game. 

In most of the cases, real world scenarios are different. Below is the initial design, I have received from the client.

In order to confirm my understanding, to have further discussion on it, to go from idea to actual prototype and then eventually to estimate, I had to come up an interactive prototype.

ionic-creator proved to be the best tool to make a rapid prototype. > Creator is a simple drag-&-drop tool for going from the idea to App Store, with just the drag of a mouse

Creator comes with practically all of the Ionic UI elements, including support for tabs, side menus, slide boxes, modals, and forms.

In 15 minutes, I was able to come up with the below video prototype. Creator is an incredible tool with zero learning curve.

No doubt this gives immensely delightful client experience and feels professional.

Only requirements needed are below.

  • Have access to a computer

  • Have internet access

  • Know how to drag-and-drop
